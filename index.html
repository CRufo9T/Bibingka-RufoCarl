Bibingka

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice flour
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 2 ½ teaspoon baking powder
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1/4 cup fresh milk
  • 1 piece salted sliced duck egg
  • 1/2 cup grated cheese
  • 3 pieces raw eggs
  • 1/4 cup grated coconut
  • pre-cut banana leaf

Instructions

  1. Step 1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Line your baking pans (or small pie tins) with banana leaves, if using. Lightly grease the banana leaves or pans with butter to prevent sticking.
  2. Step 2. In a large bowl, sift together the rice flour, all-purpose flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar. In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs, coconut milk, fresh milk, and melted butter until well combined. Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, mixing until smooth and free of lumps.
  3. Step 3. Pour the batter into the prepared pans, filling them about 3/4 full to allow room for the bibingka to rise. Arrange slices of salted egg and grated cheese on top of the batter.
  4. Step 4. Place the pans in the preheated oven and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the bibingka is golden brown and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. For a charred effect, you can broil the bibingka for an additional 2-3 minutes, watching closely to avoid burning.
  5. step 5 Remove the bibingka from the oven and let it cool slightly. Brush the top with melted butter and sprinkle with grated fresh coconut, if desired. Serve warm with a cup of coffee or hot chocolate.
